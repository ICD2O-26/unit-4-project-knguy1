Self-Driving Cars

What are self-driving cars?

Self-driving cars are vehicles that can drive themselves without a human driver.
They use cameras, sensors, and AI to understand the road.

How do they work?

The car collects data from sensors and cameras.
Then it uses AI to make decisions like stopping and turning.

Where are they used?

They are used in transportation and are being tested by companies.
They could be used for taxis and deliveries.

Impacts

Impacts Table

Positive Negative
Fewer accidents Job loss
Safer roads Technology errors

Self-driving cars can reduce accidents and make travel easier.
However, they may cause job loss and have safety risks.
